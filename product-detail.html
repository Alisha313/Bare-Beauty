Application Steps

1

Cleanse

Start with a clean, dry face. Use your favorite cleanser to remove makeup and impurities.

2

Apply Serum

Dispense 4-5 drops onto fingertips. Gently pat and press into skin, avoiding the eye area.

3

Wait

Allow 1-2 minutes for the serum to fully absorb before applying other products.

4

Moisturize & Protect

Follow with your moisturizer. In the morning, always apply SPF as Vitamin C can increase sun sensitivity.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Use in the morning for best antioxidant protection
  • Store in a cool, dark place to maintain potency
  • If new to Vitamin C, start every other day
  • Pair with SPF for maximum results
